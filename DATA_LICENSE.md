**What this covers and what it doesn't:**

- These files are ParaMat's own compilation: cross-source **consensus
  statistics** (medians, ranges, source counts and spreads) computed over
  multiple openly licensed upstreams, plus community-contributed rows with
  per-row provenance.
- No verbatim slicer profile files are contained here or redistributed.
