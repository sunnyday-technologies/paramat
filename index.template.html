Conditions = cross-source consensus of open slicer + manufacturer data (OFD·MIT, Orca/Prusa/Bambu·AGPL, Cura·CC0). Temperature is a range — strength picks the max (higher nozzle temp → stronger layer bond; ~+20 °C headroom above optimal before quality fails, per 948k CAXTON frames). RFID export: OpenPrintTag (MIT) and OpenSpool JSON. Not measured optima; single-source materials are flagged.

Max volumetric flow is also a hotend limit. Verify with a quick extrude-in-air test at rising rates: the strand should coil out soft (fully melted); if it comes out stiff/straight you're past the melt capacity — slow down, run hotter, or fit a high-flow hotend. Method: Ellis' Print Tuning Guide.
